Project Gita began with a simple desire: to help my mother prepare for her Shlokanka examination conducted by Gita Parivar.

What started as a tool for one mother has grown into a comprehensive repository of all 700 verses and 18 pushpikas of the Shrimad Bhagavad Gita, designed for anyone seeking to master these timeless verses.

We acknowledge Gita Parivar and the esteemed authors of the translated texts for their inspiration.
